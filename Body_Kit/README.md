# Bodykit

This folder contains **custom bodywork components** developed for the **Toyota Cynos / Toyota Paseo Project**.

The parts included in this section are intended to enhance the vehicle’s **aesthetic appearance** and may include:

- Custom spoilers  
- Exterior add-on parts  
- Experimental or prototype body components  
- 3D-printable parts and related documentation  

⚠️ **Disclaimer**  
All components are provided for **experimental and personal use**.  
Fitment, durability, and legality may vary depending on the vehicle and local regulations.  
Always verify measurements and regulations before installation.
